Welcome to my blog!

My name is Hayden McGuire, I am a rising 2L law student at Suffolk Univserity Law School, who is dedicated to working on creating a more equitable legal system. I want to learn to use technology to further this goal.

I am currently working with the Trial Court Service Center's in Massachusetts using a virtual intake system to expedite access to legal information and assistance to self represented litigants. In tandem, I am working with the Legal Inovation and Technologies Lab at Suffolk University to create tools for that same SRL population.

My path to Law School was unconventional. Initially apprehensive to formal education, and set on a the path to work in a trade, I did not see the legal field in my futre. After seeing my coworkers in the trades have their bodies start to fail, and the daily pain i felt at only 20 years old, I decided that I needed to change paths. I intially went to Community College to study computer networking, the two networking classes I took where the begining and end of my short exposure to computer science in the classroom environment. During my time at MassBay Community College I worked as a tutor; this is when my interest in the legal field started to crystalize. After getting an Assocaites Degree I attended Suffolk University's undergaduate school to get study, and get a degree in, Politics Philosophy and Economics.
